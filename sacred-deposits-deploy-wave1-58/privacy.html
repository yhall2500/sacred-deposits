Privacy Policy

Effective: May 25, 2026 · Last updated: May 15, 2026

Pre-publication note. This privacy policy has been drafted to standard California-compliant practice and reflects how EQGenix Inc. handles personal information across SacredDeposits.com and related properties. This document must be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before publication. Last attorney review: [pending].

EQGenix Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you visit SacredDeposits.com, EQGenix.com, related sites, and our products and services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you give us directly — for example, when you subscribe to our email list, purchase a product, reserve a place in The Vault, register for a workshop, or contact us. This may include:

  • Name and email address
  • Mailing address (for physical products)
  • Payment information (processed by our payment partners; we do not store full card numbers)
  • Communications you send us
  • Survey or assessment responses (e.g., the EWD-1™ instrument)

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our sites, we automatically collect certain information through cookies and similar technologies:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Pages viewed, time spent, referring site
  • Device identifiers and approximate location

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver the books, products, and services you request
  • Send launch updates, founders' communications, and the publications you subscribed to
  • Improve our sites, programs, and editorial output
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited information with:

  • Service providers who process payments, ship books, send emails, or host our sites — only as necessary to provide those services
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by law
  • Successors in the event of a business transfer

4. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data in portable format
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time (unsubscribe link in every email)

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including the right to know, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our sites use cookies for essential functionality (cart, login, language preference) and analytics (page views, traffic sources). You can disable cookies in your browser, though some features may not work without them.

6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission over the internet is fully secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, though we do all we reasonably can.

7. Children's Privacy

Our sites and services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. International Visitors

Our Services are operated from the United States. If you access our Services from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S., where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be communicated by email or by a prominent notice on the site.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or our handling of your personal information can be sent to our contact page or directly to EQGenix Inc., Southern California.
