The Vault.

Where the frameworks become practice. Assessments, protocols, monthly conversations with Yvonne — and the quiet company of couples doing the long work.

Reserve Your Place What's Inside

The book gives you the language. The Vault gives you the room.

This is the private practice space where the Sacred Deposits™ frameworks stop being a chapter and start being a Tuesday morning — the assessments you run with your spouse, the protocols you walk together, and the conversations you have when the kitchen table needs more than another podcast.

The Vault is for the marriages that decided to be early. The ones not waiting for the breakthrough — just willing to make the deposit, today, on Tuesday morning.

What's Inside

The Full Practice Stack.

The diagnostic instrument. Sixteen questions, eight minutes — a clear read on which deposits are compounding and which are quietly costing you.

The Three Protocols

The 30-Day Reset™, the Covenant Audit™, and the Legacy Ledger™ — full guided sequences, not summaries.

Guided Practices

The Private Group

The Founder's Note
