The Inevitable

Every marriage has an account.
The Overdraft Principle™ is what happens
when you stop making deposits.

Most marriages don't end in betrayal. They erode in the silent withdrawals: the unkind word never repaired, the gentle answer never given, the conversation never had. Each one signs an invisible IOU. Each one comes due.

Sacred Deposits™ is the language for the discipline that protects against that erosion — and the framework for the marriage you'd be willing to compound for thirty years.

About the Author

Yvonne G. Hall

Applied behavioral scientist. Wife of twenty-nine years. Founder of EQGenix.

Yvonne G. Hall is an applied behavioral scientist and the founder of EQGenix Inc., a Christ-centered emotional wealth advisory. She has spent two decades quietly building the frameworks that became Sacred Deposits™ — first in her own marriage, then in the marriages of the women she counseled, and finally on the page.

She lives in Southern California with her husband Jerry, with whom she has been married twenty-nine years. She is the mother of seven and grandmother of twelve. This is her first book.
