For Immediate Release

Applied Behavioral Scientist Yvonne G. Hall to Release Sacred Deposits™, a Christ-Centered Marriage Framework Built on the 5:1 Ratio

Canyon Lake, CA — May 25, 2026

Yvonne G. Hall, founder and CEO of EQGenix Inc., releases Sacred Deposits™: The Blueprint for Protecting Your Marriage on May 25, 2026 — a Christ-centered framework that translates decades of relationship research into a working discipline Christian couples can actually run.

At the framework's center is what Hall calls the 5:1 ratio: the research-backed pattern that stable marriages produce roughly five small deposits — moments of repair, attention, and tenderness — for every one withdrawal. Sacred Deposits™ takes that ratio out of the laboratory and into the covenant, naming behaviors most couples cannot articulate but feel every day.

Hall developed the framework across twenty-nine years of marriage to Jerry Pollard-Hall, while raising seven children and welcoming twelve grandchildren. A former Guardian ad Litem in Florida and California and NAMA CAMS-II certified, she brings together behavioral science, scripture, and lived experience in a voice that reads as neither clinical nor sentimental.

The hardcover ($36.99, 384 pages) launches May 25, 2026, followed by paperback, audiobook (read by the author), and Kindle eBook editions. A Spanish edition, Depósitos Sagrados, is scheduled for Fall 2026.

Sacred Deposits™ is published by EQGenix Inc., a Christ-centered Emotional Wealth Advisory firm operating under the positioning Build Emotional Wealth™. Hall is also the founder of Life in Darkness, a 501(c)(3) human-capacity development organization, and a federally certified WOSB co-founder of YGH Technology Consultants.

Talking Points

The core message

Marriage runs on deposits, not destiny.

Most marriage advice tells couples what to feel. Sacred Deposits™ gives them what to do — small, structured, repeated actions that compound over time.

The 5:1 ratio.

Research-backed: it takes five deposits to repair one withdrawal. Yvonne teaches couples to track both — and to live above the ratio.

The marriage your children inherit.

“You are not raising children. You are raising future spouses.” The Legacy Ledger™ framework names what gets transferred — for good or for harm — across generations.

The 30-Day Reset Protocol™.

A built-in restoration practice. Three phases (Notice, Practice, Compound), thirty days, applicable to year-three or year-thirty marriages, with or without your spouse's participation.

Christ-centered, science-backed.

Yvonne weaves applied behavioral science with scripture in a way Christian readers can use without feeling preached at, and that secular readers can engage with without feeling proselytized.

An interracial covenant at the center.

The framework was built across twenty-nine years of marriage to Jerry Pollard-Hall, an interracial Christian marriage — lending the work a positioning few marriage authors can claim.

An entire ecosystem, not a single book.

Beyond the hardcover: his/her journals, a couples workbook, a premarital curriculum, a 501(c)(3) outreach arm, court-ordered curricula, and an institutional partnership pilot with Canyon Lake PD launching August 2026.

Background

For context

About EQGenix Inc.

EQGenix Inc. is a Christ-centered Emotional Wealth Advisory firm operating under the positioning Build Emotional Wealth™ and the tagline Built, Not Downloaded™.

The company operates across five verticals: publishing (Sacred Deposits™ and its ecosystem), institutional partnerships (Sovereign Command™ family with municipal police and fire departments), court-ordered curricula (EQGenix Institute LLC), professional certification (Formation Coach Certification), and community programming through Life in Darkness, its 501(c)(3) partner.

EQGenix Inc. is headquartered in Canyon Lake, California, and qualified to do business in California as a Delaware C-Corp.

About Yvonne G. Hall

Yvonne is a Black Christian woman, married for twenty-nine years to Jerry Pollard-Hall (an interracial couple), mother of seven, grandmother of twelve. She holds NAMA CAMS-II certification and is a former Guardian ad Litem in both Florida and California.

She is the founder & CEO of EQGenix Inc., founder & Executive Director of Life in Darkness (501(c)(3)), and co-founder of YGH Technology Consultants (a federally certified WOSB / EDWOSB).

Her father, George Edward Spearman, and mother, Yvonne Louise Vaughan Spearman, both passed; their loss informs her work on covenant memory, inheritance, and the practice of staying.
