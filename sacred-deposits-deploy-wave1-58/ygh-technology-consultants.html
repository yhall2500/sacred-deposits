YGH Technology Consultants.

A federally certified woman-owned small business. Technology and consulting practice serving public-sector clients, law-enforcement agencies, and government partners.

YGH Technology Consultants delivers technology and advisory services to the institutions that hold the line — government agencies, law enforcement, public-sector organizations, and the contractors who serve them.

The practice was built on a simple proposition: that federal-grade rigor and human-grade thoughtfulness belong in the same room. Most contractors deliver one or the other. YGH delivers both, with the small-business agility and certified diversity status that opens specific federal procurement pathways.

Co-founded by Yvonne G. Hall and Jerry Pollard-Hall, the firm operates at the intersection of applied behavioral science, organizational systems, and the technology infrastructure that keeps public-sector institutions functioning.
